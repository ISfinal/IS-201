Work sample by Natalie Bodine

This last summer I worked as an event planner for the Colonial Heritage Festival, a branch under the Utah Freedom Festival. It is the largest reinactment West of the Mississippi river. Participants and reinactors come from accross the nation to experience the Colonial United States unlike ever before.

My responsibilities included everything from running social media accounts, organizing volunteers, and planning interactive acitivities for the festival. I worked directly under the Chair of the festival and the head of the Colonial Heritage Foundation board.

Below is an example of some of the design work I completed as part of my internship. These are email signature and header examples for our staff members to add professionalism to their emails.


I enjoyed my time with the festival and appreciate the many learning opportunities that it provided for me. To learn more about the Colonial Heritage Festival, visit their website at festival.colonialheritage.org and like them on Facebook.
